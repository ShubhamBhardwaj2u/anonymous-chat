Safety Options

Guidelines for Safe Chatting:

  • Privacy & Anonymity: Keep chats anonymous. Never share personal details like names, addresses, phone numbers, emails, photos, or identifiable info.
  • Safety First: Avoid sharing financial details, links, or anything that could risk your security. Report suspicious users immediately.
  • Respect & Conduct: Be polite and kind. No harassment, bullying, hate speech, spam, or inappropriate content (e.g., explicit, violent, or illegal topics).
  • Age & Content Restrictions: This app is for users 18+. Do not engage in or share adult content, and avoid interacting with minors.
  • Usage Rules: Keep conversations appropriate and fun. Excessive trolling, flooding, or rule-breaking may result in temporary blocks.
  • Reporting & Feedback: Use "Report User" for issues. Your feedback helps us improve—share it via the feedback option.
  • Spread the Word: Enjoying the app? Share it with friends to help grow our community!
